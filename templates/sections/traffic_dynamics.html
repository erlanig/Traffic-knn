Decode Traffic Dynamics

Decode the intricacies of traffic patterns by categorizing predictions into different levels: Low, Normal, High, or Heavy, helping you comprehend the nuances of road activity.

1. Low

Road lanes may be relatively empty, vehicles can move easily without obstacles, and average speeds may be higher.

2. Normal

The road is full of vehicles, but the traffic flow remains smooth without significant congestion.

3. High

Road lanes may be full of vehicles, and average speed may decrease. The possibility of traffic jams and increased travel time may occur.

4. Heavy

Road lanes may be crowded with vehicles, average speeds may slow significantly, and travel times may become longer. There may be a complete stop (congestion) or severe traffic jams.
